About our Company

We have been in business for over 30 years and are dedicated to creating high quality products and giving superior service. We supply over 40 dealers across Alberta, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba. Our talented, long-term staff have demonstrated repeatedly that we're capable of building over 1500 hopper cones annually. Ranging from 12ft in diameter to 33ft, their isn't a hopper cone we can't build for you reguardless of your preffered type of grain bin. All of our hopper cones are designed by our partnered company Method Engineering & Design inc.
