U-Dumps

The U-DUMP is designed with very little hitch weight and can be towed behind most vehicles. Not only very well balanced, but also safe and easy to use! With a simple turn of the winch handle you can dump up to the full 2,800 pound capacity rating in just seconds! The U-DUMP comes with 2 inch deep rib-stiffened industrial plastic lids, removable double pin security hitch, easy release locking pins, and a simple winch controlled dumping system.

What can a U-DUMP be used for?

  • Construction Site Cleanup
  • Roofing Jobs (shingles)
  • Weddings or Yard Parties
  • Ball Tournaments
  • Home Renovations
  • Livestock Feed

The U-DUMP is also Excellent for:

  • Rural Municipality
  • Provincial Parks
  • Campgrounds
  • Trailer Courts
  • Acreages

Dump all your troubles away with the versitile and dependable U-DUMP!
